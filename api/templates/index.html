What happens under the hood

Summarized from the project README.

  • YOLO human detector crops the player; MediaPipe Pose feeds neutral vs swing gating.
  • Sequence shot classifier labels forehand, backhand, slice/volley, serve/overhead.
  • Ball detector tracks position and draws a decaying trail for trajectory context.
  • Court detector + homography project the ball onto a 200×400 mini-court overlay.
  • Pixel-to-meter conversion estimates ball speed; annotated frames render to MP4.
  • Output is uploaded to Cloudflare R2 and returned as a time-limited signed link.

Models & assets

Runtime weights loaded by the API.

  • yolo11n.pt — player detector for pose cropping.
  • hugging_face_best.pt — tennis ball detector.
  • src/court_model/best.pt — court bounding box detector.
  • shot_classifier.keras — pose-based stroke classifier.
  • neutrality.keras — neutral vs swing gate.
